# EP-12 — AI Features

> **Status:** Draft  
> **Sprint:** Future (Sprint 12+)  
> **Last updated:** 2026-07-19

---

## Business Objective

Leverage AI to enhance the platform with intelligent lawyer matching, a legal assistant chatbot, consultation summaries, document analysis, and real-time translation — creating long-term competitive differentiation.

## Scope

- AI lawyer matching (recommend lawyers based on user needs)
- AI legal assistant (chatbot for initial legal guidance)
- AI consultation summary (auto-generate summaries after consultations)
- AI document analysis (analyze legal documents)
- AI real-time translation (translate during consultations)

## Business Rules

- **No legal advice from AI**: AI assistant provides general information only, with clear disclaimers
- **Matching transparency**: AI recommendations based on specialization, availability, rating, and language — explainable
- **Consultation summaries**: generated after consultation completion, shared with both parties
- **Privacy**: AI features must comply with data protection regulations
- **Opt-in**: users can opt out of AI features
