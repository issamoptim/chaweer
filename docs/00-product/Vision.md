# Chaweer — Product Vision

> **Status:** In Progress  
> **Last updated:** 2026-07-19

---

## What is Chaweer?

Chaweer is a premium legal platform in Morocco that connects citizens and businesses with verified lawyers for video and audio consultations. The platform is bilingual (FR / العربية / EN), mobile-first, and designed to make legal expertise accessible, trustworthy, and seamless.

## Problem Statement

Access to legal expertise in Morocco is fragmented. Citizens struggle to find verified lawyers, compare specializations, and book consultations. Lawyers lack a professional digital presence to attract and manage clients efficiently.

## Solution

A unified platform where:

- **Citizens** search a verified directory of lawyers, compare profiles, book consultations, and pay securely — all in one place.
- **Lawyers** create a professional profile, manage availability, conduct video/audio consultations, and receive payments — with a streamlined onboarding and verification process.
- **Administrators** verify lawyer credentials, moderate content, and oversee platform operations.

## Key Differentiators

- **Verified lawyers only** — bar association credentials checked before profiles go public.
- **Video-first consultations** — the core value proposition is face-to-face legal advice, online.
- **Bilingual + RTL** — French, Arabic (RTL), and English from day one.
- **Premium UX** — design-system-driven, WCAG AA accessible, mobile-first.
- **Morocco-focused** — tailored to the Moroccan legal landscape (bar associations, jurisdictions, local payment methods).

## Target Users

| User | Role | Primary Need |
|---|---|---|
| Citizen / Business | Client | Find a verified lawyer and book a consultation |
| Lawyer (Avocat) | Professional | Build a digital presence and manage client consultations |
| Admin | Operator | Verify professionals and maintain platform trust |

## MVP Definition

The MVP delivers the end-to-end flow from lawyer onboarding to citizen consultation:

1. **Professional onboarding** — lawyers register, submit credentials, get verified
2. **Public directory** — citizens search and find verified lawyers
3. **Professional profiles** — lawyers showcase expertise, availability, and pricing
4. **Appointment booking** — citizens book and conduct video consultations
5. **Payments** — secure payment for consultations
6. **Basic notifications** — email and push for appointment lifecycle
7. **Admin verification** — gate ensuring only verified lawyers are public

## Post-MVP & Future

- Reviews & ratings
- Questions & Answers community
- Enhanced notifications (in-app center, SMS)
- Full admin suite (analytics, dispute management)
- AI features (lawyer matching, legal assistant, consultation summaries)
- Subscription plans for professionals
