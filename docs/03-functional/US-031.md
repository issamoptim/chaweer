## Description

As a lawyer, I want to select my areas of legal specialization and the languages I speak so that citizens can find me based on their needs.

## Acceptance Criteria (Draft)

- Specializations: multi-select from predefined list of legal areas (family law, business law, criminal law, real estate, labor law, immigration, etc.)
- At least one specialization required
- Languages: multi-select from predefined list (Arabic, French, English, Amazigh, Spanish, etc.)
- At least one language required (beyond the UI language)
- Selections stored and displayed on professional profile
- Selections used for directory filtering (EP-04)
