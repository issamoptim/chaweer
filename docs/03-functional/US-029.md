## Description

As a lawyer, I want to submit my identity and bar association credentials so that Chaweer can verify I am a licensed legal professional.

## Acceptance Criteria (Draft)

- Lawyer provides: full legal name, bar card/registration number, bar association name, jurisdiction
- Bar association selected from a predefined list of Moroccan bar associations
- Bar card number validated for format (not for authenticity — that's admin's role in US-034)
- Information is stored and linked to the professional account
- Status set to "pending verification" after submission
- Lawyer cannot appear in public directory until verified (D-006)
